HOW TO MAKE SOFT CHAPATI

Chapati is a popular Indian flat bread made with wheat flour. Easy chapati recipe that anyone can make at home that yields soft and puffy chapati.

Ingredients needed in making chapati

  • 2 cups of whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 tbsp of oil

Steps to follow

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt.
  2. Gradually add the warm water to the mixture and knead the dough until it becomes smooth and soft.
  3. Add 1 tbsp of oil to the dough and knead it again for a minute.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls and roll them out into thin, circular shapes
  6. Heat a tawa or griddle on medium-high heat.
  7. Place the rolled-out chapati on the tawa and cook for 30 seconds or until bubbles start to form.
  8. Flip the chapati and cook the other side for another 30 seconds or until light brown spots appear.
  9. Remove the chapati from the tawa and brush it with the remaining oil.
  10. Repeat the process with the remaining dough balls.
  11. Serve hot with your favorite curry or dal. Enjoy!
